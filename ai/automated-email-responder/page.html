This automated email responder was created to simplify and optimize business communication processes. Using Make.com, a no-code automation platform, the system integrates OpenAI's ChatGPT API for intelligent email drafting and Airtable for data retrieval and tracking. The workflow includes: Monitoring incoming emails. Processing and analyzing the content. Searching for relevant information in Airtable databases. Drafting smart, context-aware replies with GPT-3.5/GPT-4. Sending polished responses back to clients, customers, or teams. This end-to-end automation significantly reduces manual effort, ensures consistency in communication, and accelerates response times.
