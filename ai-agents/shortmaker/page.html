FAQ

Frequently asked quesions

What is ShortMaker and how does it work?

ShortMaker is an AI-powered tool that transforms your long-form videos into engaging, ready-to-publish YouTube Shorts. It automatically detects viral moments, applies smart captions, chooses optimal layouts, and exports high-quality vertical videos, all in a few clicks.

Do I need editing experience to use ShortMaker?

Not at all. ShortMaker is designed for everyone, from solo creators to marketing teams. Its intuitive interface and AI automation make it easy to generate professional-grade Shorts without any prior video editing skills.

What video formats and platforms does it support?

You can upload most common formats (MP4, MOV, etc.) and ShortMaker outputs videos optimized for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and other vertical video platforms.

Can I customize the video before exporting?

Yes. You have full control over layout (Full Frame, Portrait, Split Screen), caption style, video segments, and watermark branding.

How much time and money can I save with ShortMaker?

On average, creators save 14+ hours per video and over $500+ in editing costs. It replaces the need for manual trimming, formatting, and captioning with a fast, scalable AI-Agent.

What’s the best way to make viral YouTube Shorts?

Use Alphabase ShortMaker AI-Powered short detection and engagement-driven layout to maximize your video's impact. Caption animations and keyword-curated clips help your shorts stand out.
